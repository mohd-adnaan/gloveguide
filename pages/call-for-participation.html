Call for Participation

You are invited to participate in a small research study about wearable haptic navigation technology for cyclists and motorcyclists, as part of the Human-Computer Interaction course in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at McGill University.

Who can participate?

You must be:

  • 18 years of age or older
  • An experienced cyclist or motorcyclist
  • Comfortable navigating while cycling or walking

What is involved?

You will be asked to:

  • Provide information as part of a screening process to verify your eligibility for the study
  • Complete a questionnaire
  • Answer some interview questions
  • Perform several tasks in a laboratory-like setting
  • Be observed in a naturalistic setting while doing an activity (more specifically, navigating through a predefined route while wearing haptic navigation gloves or bracelets)
  • Perform several navigation tasks in a controlled setting
  • Be video-recorded while participating in the above activity

What is the time commitment?

We expect to conduct 4 iterations of testing throughout the academic semester, each session lasting approximately 30 minutes. However, you may agree to participate in only one or more of these sessions, as you prefer.

Is there compensation?

No, we regret we are unable to compensate you.
